Covid-19(Till 07/22/2020)

Though the first case of Covid-19 was reported back in 2019, it has started impacted from mid March, 2020 around the world. Today US, Brazil, India, Russia and South Africa have most number of confirmed cases.

This line graph helps to find out the pattern of growing number of Covid 19 confirmed cases for those 5 countries

It has five lines one for each country. Color legends are added for visual affordance. The graphs load in the page in an animated way. It is clearly visible that US is in 2nd wave and other countries in the top five list are still in fisrt wave. This is marked by annotation as well.

Following observations can be easily inferred:

1. US is in already 2nd wave as the line curve representing the total number of confirmed cases was having flattening trend around mid-May, 2020 and it is again gaining its momentum since mid-June, 2020.

2. Brazil, India and South Africa have not reached to its first peak yet as the line curves representing the total number of confirmed cases are showing continuous upward trend with no sign of flattening of the curve.

3. The line curve representing total number of confirmed cases for Russia is showing the sign of flattening the curve. It is still in first wave though.
